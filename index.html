Our Story

About Gloss

Clean finish. Sharp service. Proper attention to detail.

At Gloss Car Wash & Detailing, we're not here to rush cars through like a production line. We're here to clean them properly.

Based in Carlingford, Gloss delivers quality hand car washing and detailing with a sharper standard, better products and more attention to detail. Whether it's a quick exterior clean, a proper inside-and-out wash, or a deeper detail, every service is built around one thing — making your car look better when it leaves than when it arrived.

We believe a car wash should be simple, honest and done with care. No confusing packages. No lazy shortcuts. Just clean cars, sharp finishes and a team that takes pride in the work.

From daily drivers to weekend cars, family SUVs, work utes and prestige vehicles, Gloss is built for owners who care about how their car looks.

"Clean finish. Sharp service. Proper attention to detail. That's Gloss."
Our Values

What We Stand For

At Gloss Car Wash & Detailing, we're not trying to be just another car wash. We're building a higher standard for how a car should be cleaned, presented and looked after. To us, a proper clean is more than soap, water and a quick wipe down. It's the way the car feels when you get back in. It's the shine on the paint, the clarity of the glass, the freshness of the interior and the confidence that the job was done properly. We stand for honest service, sharper results and real pride in the work.

Proper hand washing. Honest service. Real attention to detail. A cleaner, sharper standard.
